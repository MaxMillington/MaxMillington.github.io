FareWell Mobile App

The FareWell mobile app is built with React Native. Because React Native is cross platform, I had the opportunity to familiarize myself with some of the differences that go into developing for IOS and Android. I also performed some of the DevOps related tasks such as setting up CI and using Fastlane to make deploying the apps much more automated.

FareWell App

FareWell is a digital lifestyle medicine app that aims to treat and prevent weight related illnesses through lifestyle change. Members increase their culinary health literacy through a series of videos, informative graphics, interactive meal planning, and interactive shopping list tools. Members are also put in touch with health coaches to help support them through the intensive process.
FareWell is built with React, Redux, React Router, and Ruby on Rails. As one of the first ten employees, I was involved in all levels of the stack and every part of the app. For example, I set up Redux throughout the app, integrating with Strip for payments, and built the architecture for accepting promo codes.
